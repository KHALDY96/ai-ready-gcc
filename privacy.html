Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 June 2026

AI Ready GCC ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you use our website and Business Efficiency Assessment, how we use it, and the choices you have. We only collect what we need to share your results and follow up about how AI could help your business.

1. Information we collect

When you use our assessment, you may provide:

  • Contact details: your full name, email address, and phone number.
  • Business details: your company name, business type, and company size.
  • Assessment answers: your responses to the questions in the Business Efficiency Assessment.

We may also automatically collect basic technical information such as your device type and how you arrived at our site (for example, from an advertisement), to help us understand and improve our marketing.

2. How we use your information

  • To calculate and share your AI readiness results.
  • To contact you about a free consultation and how AI could help your business.
  • To respond to your enquiries and provide the services you request.
  • To understand which businesses find our assessment useful, so we can improve it.

We will only contact you because you asked for your results and agreed to be contacted. You can ask us to stop at any time.

3. How your information is stored

Your information is stored securely using trusted third-party business tools. We use Airtable to store submissions and Make to transfer your form data into Airtable. These providers maintain their own security measures. We restrict access to your information to people who need it to operate this service.

4. Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with:

  • Service providers who help us run this service (for example, Airtable and Make), under their respective terms.
  • Advertising platforms such as Meta (Facebook and Instagram) and Google, which may use cookies or similar technologies to measure and improve our advertising. These platforms process data under their own privacy policies.
  • Authorities, if required by law.

5. Cookies and advertising

We may run advertisements on Meta and other platforms. These platforms may place cookies or tracking pixels to measure ad performance and show relevant ads. You can manage cookies through your browser settings and your ad preferences within each platform.

6. How long we keep your information

We keep your information only for as long as needed to provide our services and follow up with you, after which it is deleted or anonymised. You may ask us to delete your information sooner.

7. Your rights

You may request to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you, and you may withdraw your consent to be contacted at any time. To make a request, contact us using the details below.

8. Children

Our service is intended for business owners and professionals. It is not directed at anyone under 18, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "last updated" date.
